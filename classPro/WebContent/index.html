Instructions | How To Use ClassPro

This is the home page where you can find some general info. The DePauw Classes page: allows you to see the DePauw classes and what each class requires in order to major or minor. The classes included do not include classes that are more interdisciplinary or require multiple departments to interact.

The Advising Page: is where you can self register courses (up to 4) you have taken which than takes you to the Form Page.

The Form Page: where you can find the registered information submited from the advising page. It is also the location where you can find your Competency, Distribution Area, Credit and GPA Tracker that is dependent on the information provided in Advising Page.
